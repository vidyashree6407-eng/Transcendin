About TranscendIN

Revolutionizing education through personalized, live instructor-led learning experiences for the modern world.

Our Story

TranscendIN was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to make world-class education accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believed that technology and passionate instructors could transform the learning experience.

Starting as a small startup in 2020, we've grown to serve thousands of students and professionals across 150+ countries. Our commitment to quality, personalization, and real results has never wavered.

Today, TranscendIN is the leading platform for live, one-on-one instructor-led courses, connecting ambitious learners with expert educators who are passionate about their craft.

Our Core Values

Passion for Learning

We believe that education is transformative. Our instructors are passionate educators who love sharing knowledge and helping students succeed.

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards in everything we do - from course quality to instructor expertise to student support. Excellence is non-negotiable.

Global Impact

We're committed to breaking barriers and making quality education accessible to learners worldwide, regardless of geography or background.

Student-Centric

Every decision we make is guided by what's best for our students. Personalization, flexibility, and support are at the heart of everything we do.

Innovation

We continually innovate to improve the learning experience through technology, pedagogy, and creative solutions to real educational challenges.

Integrity

We operate with transparency and honesty. Our commitment to integrity builds trust with students, instructors, and partners alike.
