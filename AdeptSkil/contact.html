Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Enrolling is simple! Visit our Courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click "Enroll Now". You can start with a free trial to get a feel for the platform and instructor style before committing.

What is the refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with your course. If you'd like a refund, please contact our support team at support@transcendin.com with your enrollment details.

Can I switch instructors if I'm not satisfied?

Yes! We understand that instructor-student fit is important. If you're not connecting with your instructor, contact our support team and we'll help you find a better match.

Is there a certificate after completion?

Absolutely! Upon successfully completing a course, you'll receive a recognized certificate that you can add to your resume and LinkedIn profile. All our certificates are verified and industry-recognized.

What are the system requirements?

You need a computer or tablet with a stable internet connection, a webcam, and a microphone. We support all major browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) on Windows, Mac, and Linux.

How do I schedule my first session?

After enrollment, you'll have access to your instructor's calendar. Choose from available time slots that work for your timezone. You can schedule sessions at your convenience, with our platform supporting 150+ countries and timezones.
